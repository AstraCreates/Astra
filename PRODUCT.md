# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Founders and small startup teams — initially solo builders at zero-to-one stage, growing into teams delegating execution to AI agents. They open Astra to launch or operate a company: spinning up goals, tracking agent runs, monitoring outreach, reviewing what the AI founding team is doing on their behalf. Context: work mode, focused, expect speed and signal over polish.

## Product Purpose

Astra is an AI founding team. From a single goal, a coordinated set of AI agents builds and operates a company — handling outreach, research, product thinking, payments, and ops. Success means the founder spends less time on execution and more time on direction. The interface is the control room: it should feel like a Bloomberg Terminal for your startup, not a chat window.

## Brand Personality

Bold, intelligent, alive. The product has real agency — it's doing things. The UI should reflect that: confident, precise, and visibly in motion without being noisy.

Reference: Superhuman — speed and craft. Keyboard-first sensibility, incredibly refined interactions, premium feel that earns trust. Not flashy; *exact*.

## Anti-references

- AI chatbot UI (ChatGPT, Claude.ai) — full-width chat, plain white, no structure, no density
- Dark-mode terminal toy — all-black + green text, hacker aesthetic for its own sake
- Warm cream/editorial — sand/paper tones, editorial typography, too soft for a work tool
- Generic SaaS (Notion/Linear clones) — rounded cards, muted gray palette, safe and forgettable

## Design Principles

1. **Speed is felt.** Interactions must feel instant. Hover states, transitions, and feedback should be sub-200ms. Latency is a design failure.
2. **The system is alive.** Agents are running. The UI should carry the energy of something actively working — motion is information, not decoration.
3. **Authority without noise.** Electric blue and sharp geometry are load-bearing brand signals. Every element earns its place or it's cut.
4. **Density serves clarity.** Information density is a feature for power users. Compress well; never hide useful data behind progressive disclosure that adds friction.
5. **Earned confidence.** No rounded-corner softness, no apologetic grays. Every surface choice should communicate that Astra knows what it's doing.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA: body text ≥4.5:1 contrast, large/bold text ≥3:1. Both light and dark themes maintained. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` on all animations.
