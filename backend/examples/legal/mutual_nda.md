# Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement
Between {{Party A}} ("Party A") and {{Party B}} ("Party B"), effective {{date}}.

**1. Purpose.** The parties wish to explore {{purpose, e.g. a potential business
relationship}} and may share confidential information.

**2. Confidential Information.** Non-public information disclosed by either party, marked
or reasonably understood as confidential, including {{business plans, financials, code,
customer data, etc.}}. Excludes info that is public, already known, independently
developed, or rightfully received from a third party.

**3. Obligations.** Each party will (a) use the other's Confidential Information only for
the Purpose, (b) protect it with at least reasonable care, and (c) not disclose it except
to employees/advisors who need it and are bound by similar duties.

**4. Term.** Obligations last {{N years, e.g. 3}} from disclosure. Trade secrets protected
as long as they remain trade secrets.

**5. Return/Destruction.** On request, each party returns or destroys the other's
Confidential Information.

**6. No license / no obligation.** No IP rights are granted. Nothing obligates either party
to proceed with a transaction.

**7. Remedies.** Breach may cause irreparable harm; injunctive relief available in addition
to other remedies.

**8. Governing law.** {{state/country}}.
