# B2B Outreach Sequence

## 7-touch sequence (2 weeks)

```
Day 1:  LinkedIn connection request (personalized note)
Day 2:  Email #1 — problem hook
Day 4:  LinkedIn message (after connection accepted)
Day 6:  Email #2 — case study / social proof
Day 8:  LinkedIn voice note (if available) or comment on their post
Day 10: Email #3 — direct ask + specific time offer
Day 14: Email #4 — breakup
```

## LinkedIn connection note (300 char limit)
```
Hi {first_name} — I work with {role}s at {company_type} companies on {pain_area}.
Saw your post about {relevant_topic} and wanted to connect. No pitch, just building my network.
```

## LinkedIn follow-up (after acceptance)
```
Thanks for connecting, {first_name}.

Curious — how's {company_name} handling {pain_point}?
I ask because we just helped {similar_company_type} cut {metric} by {X}%.

Happy to share what worked if useful.
```

## Discovery call framework (30 min)
```
0-5 min:   Rapport + confirm time
5-15 min:  Discovery questions:
           - "Walk me through how you currently handle {process}"
           - "What does it cost you when {pain_point} happens?"
           - "Who else is involved in solving this?"
           - "What have you tried before?"
15-20 min: Demo — show ONLY the features that solve their stated pain
20-25 min: Objection handling
25-30 min: Close or define next step
```

## Objection responses

**"Too expensive"**
```
I hear you. What's the cost of doing nothing — in time, errors, or lost deals?
Most customers find {product_name} pays for itself in {X weeks/months}.
Can I show you an ROI calculation based on your numbers?
```

**"Not the right time"**
```
What would make the timing better?
If it's budget, our {smaller_plan} at ${X}/mo might be a lower-stakes way to start.
When should I follow up?
```

**"Need to think about it"**
```
Of course. What specific questions do you want to think through?
I want to make sure you have everything you need to decide.
Can we schedule 15 min next {day} for your questions?
```

**"Need to involve my team"**
```
Who else would be part of this decision?
I'd love to run a quick demo for your whole team — I can tailor it to their questions.
Would {day} work for a 30-min group call?
```

## CRM hygiene (per lead)
- Log every touchpoint with date + outcome
- Set follow-up task immediately after every interaction
- Move to "Closed Lost" with reason after breakup email — no ghosts
- Win/loss reasons: price, timing, competitor, feature gap, budget frozen, champion left
