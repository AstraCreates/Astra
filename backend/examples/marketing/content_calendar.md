A 4-week starter content plan built on pillars. Replace `{{...}}`. Reuse the structure
weekly; rotate pillars.

## Step 1 — Pick 3–4 content pillars
Pillars = the themes you want to own. Derive from ICP pains + product capabilities.
e.g. {{pillar_1: how-to/education}}, {{pillar_2: industry trends}},
{{pillar_3: customer stories}}, {{pillar_4: product/use-cases}}.

## Step 2 — Weekly cadence (lean, sustainable)
- 1 long-form (blog/SEO, 1000–1500 words) → targets a keyword.
- 3 short social posts (repurpose the blog into hooks/threads).
- 1 email to list (digest or single idea).

## Step 3 — 4-week calendar
Week 1: Pillar 1 — "{{how_to_topic}}" (blog) + 3 social repurposes + email.
Week 2: Pillar 2 — "{{trend_topic}}" (blog) + 3 social + email.
Week 3: Pillar 3 — "{{customer_story}}" (case study) + 3 social + email.
Week 4: Pillar 4 — "{{use_case}}" (product-led blog) + 3 social + email.

## Per-post brief template
- Working title: {{title}}
- Target keyword / angle: {{keyword}}
- Audience + pain: {{ICP}} struggling with {{pain}}
- One takeaway: {{single_idea}}
- CTA: {{next_step}}

## Repurposing rule (1 → many)
Blog → 1 X/LinkedIn thread (the H2s as the hooks) → 1 carousel → 1 email → 1 short video script.

Rules:
- Each piece earns its place by serving ONE pillar + ONE ICP pain.
- SEO posts: answer the query in the first 100 words, then expand.
- Don't start from a blank page — clone last week's brief and swap the topic.
