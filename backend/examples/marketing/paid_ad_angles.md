Reusable ad angles + copy templates for Meta/Google/TikTok. Pick 3–5 angles, write 2
variants each, test. Replace `{{...}}`.

## The 6 angles (test across these, not random variations)
1. Pain/Problem — "Still {{painful_status_quo}}? There's a faster way."
2. Outcome/Aspiration — "{{dream_result}} in {{timeframe}}."
3. Social proof — "{{N}} {{ICP}} switched to {{product}}. Here's why."
4. Objection-crush — "Think {{objection}}? {{counter_proof}}."
5. Mechanism/Unique — "The {{unique_method}} that makes {{outcome}} possible."
6. Comparison — "{{product}} vs {{alternative}}: {{key_difference}}."

## Meta primary-text template
Hook (line 1, the scroll-stopper): {{angle_hook}}
Body: 2–3 short lines — agitate the pain, present the shift, name the outcome.
Proof: 1 stat or testimonial.
CTA: {{action}} → {{link}}
Headline (under image): {{outcome}} {{qualifier}}
Description: {{risk_remover}}

## Google Search (RSA) assets
Headlines (15, ≤30 chars each): mix outcome, brand, offer, CTA, objection.
  e.g. "{{Outcome}} Fast", "{{Product}} for {{ICP}}", "Try Free Today", "No {{pain}}"
Descriptions (4, ≤90 chars): expand outcome + proof + CTA.

## Creative direction (for the designer/asset)
- UGC-style > polished for cold prospecting.
- Show the product in use or the before→after.
- Text overlay = the hook only, big and readable on mute.

Rules:
- One angle per ad set so you learn what resonates.
- First 3 words carry the click. Lead with the prospect, not the product.
- Match the ad's promise to the landing page hero exactly (message match).
