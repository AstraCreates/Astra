# Cold Email Templates

## B2B SaaS — Problem-led (3-email sequence)

### Email 1: Problem hook
Subject: `{first_name}, question about {pain_point}`

```
Hi {first_name},

Quick question — how do you currently handle {specific_pain_point}?

Most {role}s at {company_type} companies tell me they're stuck doing it manually.
We built {product_name} to fix that — cuts {pain_point} from {old_time} to {new_time}.

Worth a 15-min chat to see if it fits?

{signature}
```

### Email 2: Social proof (3 days later)
Subject: `Re: question about {pain_point}`

```
Hi {first_name},

Wanted to follow up and share what {similar_company} did after using {product_name}:

"{one_sentence_result}"

Happy to walk you through how we'd do the same for {company_name}.

15 min this week?

{signature}
```

### Email 3: Breakup (5 days later)
Subject: `Closing the loop`

```
Hi {first_name},

I'll stop reaching out after this — I know you're busy.

If {pain_point} is something you revisit later, here's a link to try {product_name} free: {link}

Either way, hope {company_name} crushes it.

{signature}
```

## Local Service — Neighborhood intro

Subject: `{first_name} — quick intro from a local business`

```
Hi {first_name},

I run {business_name} in {neighborhood} — we do {service_type}.

I noticed you're also running a local business nearby, and I wanted to introduce myself.

If you ever need {service} or know someone who does, I'd love to be your go-to.

We offer: {offer_1}, {offer_2}, and {offer_3}.

Book a free {consultation/quote}: {booking_link}

{signature}
{phone}
```

## E-commerce — Influencer/collab outreach

Subject: `Collab idea for {creator_name}'s audience`

```
Hi {first_name},

I've been following your content — {specific_genuine_compliment}.

I run {brand_name}, {one_line_brand_description}.

I think your audience would love {product} because {specific_reason_relevant_to_their_audience}.

Would you be open to trying it? Happy to send samples with zero obligation.

If it fits your vibe, we could explore: affiliate (20% commission), gifted collab, or paid post.

No pressure either way — just wanted to reach out.

{signature}
```

## Rules for effective cold email
- Subject line under 40 chars, no spam words (free, guaranteed, $$)
- First line must NOT start with "I" or "My company"
- One clear CTA per email, not multiple asks
- Personalize the first line with a genuine observation
- Never claim: testimonials, revenue, or ratings you don't have
- Follow CAN-SPAM: real address in footer + unsubscribe link
